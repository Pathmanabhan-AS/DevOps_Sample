Kabaddi Overview

What is Kabaddi?

Kabaddi is a team sport that originated in ancient India. The game involves two teams of seven players each. The objective is to score points by raiding the opponent's half and tagging as many of their players as possible, while holding one's breath and chanting "kabaddi".

Popular Kabaddi Teams

  • Pakistan
  • Bangladesh
